Our Core Solutions

Addressing key challenges in fish farming for higher productivity

Premium Fingerlings Supply

We solve the problem of smallholder farmers receiving underweight, immature fingerlings (<1g) that result in over 50% mortality rates.

  • Large-size (5g+) monosex Tilapia and Catfish fingerlings
  • 99% male Tilapia through successful sex reversal
  • Raised to 10g before delivery for higher survival
  • Produced using Recirculation Aquaculture Systems (RAS)

Technical Support & Consultancy

Most smallholder farmers lack training and expert support, resulting in poor farm performance and low yields.

  • In-person, on-farm consulting services

  • Sales planning and market linkage support

Feed Optimization

About Jambo Aqua Tech

Our Story

Founded in November 2020 and based on the shores of Lake Victoria in Bondo, Siaya County, Jambo Aqua Tech is committed to revolutionizing fish farming through modern hatchery systems and sustainable aquaculture practices.

Our strategic location gives us direct access to clean water and proximity to 70% of Kenya's cage farmers, allowing us to provide cost-effective solutions with minimal transportation costs.

We serve smallholder and commercial farmers across Western Kenya, helping them increase productivity, reduce losses, and realize higher profits through our comprehensive aquaculture solutions.
